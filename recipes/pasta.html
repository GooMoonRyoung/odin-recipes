Pasta

Pasta is a type of food made from a mixture of flour, water, and sometimes eggs. It comes in a variety of shapes and sizes and is a staple food in many countries, particularly in Italy where it originated. Some popular pasta shapes include spaghetti, fettuccine, penne, linguine, and elbow macaroni. Pasta is usually boiled for a few minutes in water and then served with a sauce or used in soups, salads, or baked dishes.


Pasta is a versatile and convenient food that is easy to prepare and can be customized to suit individual tastes. It is also a nutritious food, as it is a good source of carbohydrates, fiber, and protein. In addition, pasta is low in fat and calories, making it a popular food choice for people who are trying to maintain a healthy diet. Pasta is also relatively inexpensive and widely available, making it a popular food choice for families and individuals on a budget.

ingredients

  • shrimp
  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Extra-virgin Olive Oil
  • Shallots and Garlic
  • White Wine
  • Lemon Juice
  • Seasonings
  • Parsley
  1. Cook the Pasta: Cook the pasta in a large pot of boiling, salted water according to the package instructions. Reserve 1 cup of the pasta cooking water, then drain the pasta and set aside.
  2. Cook the Shrimp: In a large skillet over medium-high heat, add the olive oil and 1 tablespoon of butter. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook until they're pink and opaque, about 2-3 minutes per side. Transfer the shrimp to a plate and set aside.
  3. Cook the Shallots and Garlic: In the same skillet, add the remaining 3 tablespoons of butter, the chopped shallots, and minced garlic. Cook until the shallots are soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add the Wine and Lemon Juice: Pour the white wine and lemon juice into the skillet and let it simmer for about 2-3 minutes, or until the liquid has reduced by half.
  5. Season the Sauce: Season the sauce with salt, pepper, and other seasonings of your choice. Stir in the reserved pasta cooking water and let the sauce simmer for another 2-3 minutes.
  6. Assemble the Pasta: Add the cooked pasta and cooked shrimp to the skillet and toss everything together with the sauce until well combined.
  7. Serve: Divide the pasta among serving plates, garnish with freshly chopped parsley, and serve. Enjoy!
